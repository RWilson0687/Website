BIO

This website chronicles my journey in the IT field. My passion for technology began early, with memorable experiences such as acquiring a pallet of Macintosh computers, dot matrix printers, and SCSI interfaces from an open-air computer market, as well as setting up my first 56k modem in the late '90s. After graduating from high school in 2006, I pursued a career in computer networking, enrolling in trade school and graduating in 2009. This led to my first IT role, and I’ve spent the past 15 years honing my skills and expanding my knowledge.
Now, I’m focused on continuing my professional growth with an eye on cybersecurity. To enhance my capabilities, I’ve started learning programming languages, beginning with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I plan to expand into Python as I prepare for the CompTIA CySA+ certification.
