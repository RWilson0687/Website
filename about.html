BIO

This website is dedicated to my journey in the I.T. field. As a hobby, I have been playing with computers since I was a kid, some highlights of which include picking up a pallet of Macintoshes with a few dot matrix printers and SCSI interfaces from an open air computer market as well as setting up my first 56k modem in the late 90's. After graduating High School in 2006, I entered trade school for Computer Networking as I felt I had a calling in this field. After graduating from trade school in 2009, I entered my first I.T. position, and here I am a decade and a half later, and looking to continue my growth and development in this field. From here, my next ambition is to move into the field of cyber security, but felt that I could benefit from learning a few programing languages. I have started with HTML, CSS, and Javascript and plan on moving to Python next as I continue to study for my CompTIA CySA+ certification.
